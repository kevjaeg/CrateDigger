Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Wax ("the App") is a mobile application that connects to the Discogs API to help you manage your vinyl record collection. This policy explains what data the App accesses, stores, and shares.

1. Data We Access

When you sign in with your Discogs account, the App accesses the following data through the Discogs API:

  • Your Discogs username and profile picture
  • Your collection and wantlist data (release titles, artists, genres, dates)
  • Your collection value (minimum, median, maximum)
  • Public release information (tracklists, marketplace prices, community stats)

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores data locally on your device to enable offline access and faster performance:

  • OAuth tokens — stored in your device's secure keychain (expo-secure-store). These allow the App to communicate with Discogs on your behalf.
  • Collection & wantlist cache — stored in a local SQLite database. This cache is refreshed within 6 hours per Discogs API Terms of Service.
  • Computed statistics — genre breakdowns, decade distributions, and growth data derived from your collection cache.

Marketplace prices are never persisted locally.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or trackers
  • We do not transmit your data to any server other than the Discogs API
  • We do not store your Discogs password — authentication is handled entirely by Discogs via OAuth

4. Camera Access

The App requests camera permission solely for barcode scanning. Camera data is processed on-device to detect barcodes and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

The App communicates exclusively with the Discogs API. No other third-party services receive your data.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Logging out within the App (Profile → Log Out), which clears all stored tokens
  • Uninstalling the App, which removes the SQLite database and all cached data
  • Revoking access on Discogs Application Settings

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact
