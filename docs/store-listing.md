## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Wax is a beautifully designed Discogs client that makes managing your record collection a pleasure. Sign in with your Discogs account and get instant access to your entire collection with large cover art, detailed release info, and powerful statistics.

COLLECTION & WANTLIST
Browse your collection and wantlist in a stunning 2-column grid with high-resolution cover art. Sort by date added, artist, title, or year. Pull down to sync with Discogs. Add or remove releases with a single tap.

BARCODE SCANNER
Point your camera at any record's barcode to instantly find it on Discogs. Add it to your collection or wantlist right from the scan result.

RELEASE DETAILS
Tap any release to see the full tracklist, labels, catalog numbers, marketplace pricing, and community stats. Rate releases 1–5 stars directly in the app — your ratings sync back to Discogs.

STATS DASHBOARD
Discover your collection from a new angle with detailed statistics:
• Genre breakdown with percentages
• Decade distribution
• Top 10 labels
• Format split (vinyl, CD, cassette, digital)
• Collection growth chart
• Total collection value (min / median / max)

SEARCH
Search the entire Discogs database with format and year range filters. Results load as you scroll with infinite pagination.

SHARE
Share any release with friends via your phone's native share sheet — complete with title, artist, and Discogs link.

DESIGNED FOR VINYL LOVERS
• Dark and light theme with system auto-detect
• Offline mode with local caching
• Haptic feedback on key interactions
• Smooth skeleton loading animations
• Smart rate limiting respects Discogs API limits

Wax uses the Discogs API but is not affiliated with, sponsored, or endorsed by Discogs.
